WELCOME TO EARLY CHILDHOOD
EDUCATION ORIENTATION COURSE!

The Early Childhood Education Orientation Course was developed by the Government of Alberta to enable early childhood educators to achieve the Level 1 - Early Childhood Educator Certificate. According to the Early Learning and Child Care Act and the Early Learning and Child Care Regulation, this certificate is the minimum training requirement for primary staff in licensed child care programs.

STEP 1 - Acceptance into the course

Your application will be confirmed within two business days. If your application is approved, you will receive an email with the information you need to access the course material, otherwise you will receive a notification stating why you were declined access. Please check your junk/spam folder for the email if you have not received any correspondence in your inbox after 3 business days.

When you have completed all 18 Sessions, please complete a Course Evaluation. You will be asked questions about the delivery and the content of the course, and about how you think you can use what you have learned. You must complete the Evaluation before you will be recommended for Certification.

STEP 4 - Receiving Your Level 1 - Early Childhood Educator Certificate

Once you have completed the course and have downloaded your Letter of Completion, you are eligible to apply for your Level 1 - Early Childhood Educator Certificate.

Plagiarism

The Alberta Early Childhood Education Orientation Course trusts in the integrity of the learner to be honest while taking the online course and tests. No learner shall submit the words, ideas or writing of another person, artificial intelligence (AI) tool, or publication as the student's own in any required exercises, assignments, or tests.
